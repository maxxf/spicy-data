
3P Delivery Dashboard Structure:
================================

1. KPI DASHBOARD TAB
   - Total Sales (with week-over-week and MTD tracking)
   - Sales from Marketing (percentage of total)
   - Total Orders and Orders from Marketing
   - Average Order Value (AOV)
   - Marketing Spend and efficiency (ROAS)
   - Net Payout tracking

2. PLATFORM ANALYSIS TAB
   - Individual platform performance (Uber Eats, DoorDash, Grubhub)
   - Commission rates and fees by platform
   - Marketing spend allocation
   - Net payout percentages
   - Sales mix distribution

3. LOCATION DASHBOARD TAB
   - Performance by restaurant location
   - Top performing platforms by location
   - Marketing efficiency by location
   - Week-over-week growth tracking

4. MARKETING CAMPAIGNS TAB
   - Promotions: Tracking discounts, BOGO, free delivery offers
   - Paid Ads: Sponsored listings, featured placements, search ads
   - ROI and ROAS calculations
   - Campaign performance metrics

5. DATA UPLOAD TEMPLATES
   - Standardized CSV formats for each platform
   - Required fields for accurate KPI calculation
   - Weekly data refresh capability
